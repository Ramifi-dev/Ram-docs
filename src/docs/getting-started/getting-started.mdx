### Connecting your wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet, Fortmatic)

#### Step 1

Go to Uniswap by clicking [this link](https://app.uniswap.org/)

![](https://gblobscdn.gitbook.com/assets%2F-MLTV3O0o155b7QOWxym%2F-MLsYOzKCzjWYbjBk7wz%2F-MLsc6doYPPn57AbpJR7%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=4ec05e36-4e2a-4ab2-b5f9-1cae2ffa9212)

#### Step 2

Connect your desired wallet by clicking connect wallet.

![](https://gblobscdn.gitbook.com/assets%2F-MLTV3O0o155b7QOWxym%2F-MLsYOzKCzjWYbjBk7wz%2F-MLscZ-Wxq8iKM_BlfTA%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=3eb5583a-955f-4475-abea-1a3b2b503bb9)

#### Step 3

Now a popup will be triggered, asking you to select your compatible wallet.
In this case we will be using MetaMask, a popular method of storing and sending ERC-20 tokens.

![](https://gblobscdn.gitbook.com/assets%2F-MLTV3O0o155b7QOWxym%2F-MLsYOzKCzjWYbjBk7wz%2F-MLscszw1SoN1VljG9wh%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=e8fe7e1a-aa36-4ea3-babc-a1b82caaf28f)

> If you don't have a wallet yet, use this [link](https://blog.pryze.com/how-to-create-a-private-wallet-with-metamask-1386255f2bf8) and learn how to create a MetaMask Wallet.

#### Step 4

A notification will be appear in MetaMask asking you to confirm a connection with Uniswap. You are going to click next, and connect.

![](https://gblobscdn.gitbook.com/assets%2F-MLTV3O0o155b7QOWxym%2F-MLsYOzKCzjWYbjBk7wz%2F-MLsdnuuq0J84l6cVlcc%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=27861208-bec2-42ac-8d73-7c0a5834bf4f)

#### Step 5

Now that weâ€™re connected to Uniswap, we are able to swap tokens or Ethereum. To do this, you have to click on the "Select a token" drop down to add the contract address of the token you want to trade (RAM in this case).

![](https://gblobscdn.gitbook.com/assets%2F-MLTV3O0o155b7QOWxym%2F-MLsf9wRRYjZL8keGkOD%2F-MLsfnkTjdLTMt9ePkuF%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=3fc37e11-83aa-4d1c-986a-57ba4dbfcb7c)

#### Step 6

Finally, Ramifi's Contract Address is pasted onto the field shown below. After the contracts address is verified, you can continue by selecting RAM and entering the amount you would like to purchase using ETH.
